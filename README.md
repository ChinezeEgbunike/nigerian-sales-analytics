# nigerian-sales-analytics
I built a Power BI dashboard analyzing 2024 Nigerian e-commerce data from Electric Sheep Africa using SQL queries to extract, transform, and aggregate sales metrics and identify key revenue trends. 

I created a total of 8 visuals depicting different metrics. My first two visuals break down revenue by city and by platform. Based on this histograms, Ilorin is the city that generates the profit in Nigeria and Jumia is the most profitable profit. I then made a pie chart that broke down delivery status of all
e-commerce orders. A little bit more than 65% of orders had been delivered, 20% of orders have been shipped, almost 10% of orders were pending and 5% of orders were cancelled. I also made a pie chart with the breakdown of the percentages of revenue generated by platform. The three platforms that generated the most profit were Jumia at 34.94%, Konga at 25.01% and Jiji at 20.04%.
